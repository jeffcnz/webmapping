Tukituki Information

In the Tukituki catchment land owners are required to meet fencing and farm environmental planning requirements set out in the Hawke's Bay Regional Council Regional Resource Management Plan. Different issues have been identified in different subcatchments. This map allows the subcatchments to be identified and summarises the issues that have been identified. To select a sub-catchment either

  • click on the map
  • use the search icon to search for places
  • select the location icon to use your current location.
